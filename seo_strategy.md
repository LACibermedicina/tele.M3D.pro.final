# SEO Strategy

## In scope
- Public homepage (`/`)
- Public marketing and informational pages (`/features`, `/documentation`, `/manual`, `/faq`)
- Public access and registration flows that are intended to be discoverable or shareable (`/login`, `/register`, `/acesso`, `/join/:token`)
- Shared public HTML shell and static SEO assets

## Public-route intent notes
- `/login` and `/register*` are public entry routes and may be indexed when they support acquisition or onboarding.
- `/acesso*` and `/join/:token` are public utility/access routes and should remain crawl-controlled and non-indexed.

## Out of scope
- Authenticated dashboard and application routes after login
- Admin-only, doctor-only, patient-only, pharmacist-only, and researcher-only pages
- API endpoints except where they directly affect crawlability or public document discovery

## Target audience
- Healthcare providers, clinics, and patients evaluating a telemedicine platform
